About Flowtype

Voice-to-text dictation that actually understands you.

What it does

Hold your hotkey, speak naturally — Flowtype transcribes your voice, cleans up the ums and grammar with AI, and pastes the result wherever you're focused. Works in Slack, Gmail, Notes, or any app on your Mac.

Hotkey → Speak → Clean text appears

Credits

Built with Tauri (Rust + WebView) and powered by OpenAI Whisper (transcription) and Groq (transcription + Llama-3.3 cleanup).
